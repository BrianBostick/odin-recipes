Buffalo Chicken Dip

Description

A spicy, creamy buffalo chicken dip to make those lazy days in front of the tv more exciting!

Ingredients

  • 2 bone-in chicken breast halves (or 4 bone-in thighs)
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 stalk celery, finely diced
  • 1 (8oz) package reduced-fat cream cheese (or regular, if you're feeling it)
  • 3/4 cup blue cheese dressing
  • 3/4 cup ranch dressing
  • 1/3 cup hot sauce (Frank's RedHot is the standard)
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Place chicken in a large saucepan; cover with water. Bring to a boil and cook until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear - about 20 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center of a breast should read at least 165F. Drain and allow chick to cool enough to handle. Pull meat from bones and skin. Shred meat and set aside.
  2. Preheat oven to 350F.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir celery in hot oil until soft. Mix in cream cheese, blue cheese dressing, and ranch dressing. Cook and stir until smooth and creamy. Stir in shredded chicken and hot sauce. Transfer to an 8x8-inch baking dish and sprinkle with shredded cheese.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until golden and bubbly - about 30 minutes.
