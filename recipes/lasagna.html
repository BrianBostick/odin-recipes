Lasagna

Description

This is how to make lasagna!

It's pretty great stuff, honestly.

You can also find the recipe here

Ingredients

Cheese Sauce

  • 15oz ricotta cheese (or 2 cups)
  • 1 large egg
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese (fresh if available)
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

Meat Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion (finely diced)
  • 3/4lb ground beef
  • 3/4lb ground Italian sausage
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 40oz marinara sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce (optional, but recommended)
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Also -

  • 12 lasagna noodles (have more in case of breakage)
  • 2.5 cups mozzarella cheese

Equipment

  • 1 9x13-inch casserole dish

Steps

Prep Work

  1. Combine the cheese filling ingredients in a medium bowl and set aside. Measure out the remaining ingredients.

The Meat Sauce

  1. Head olive oil over medium heat and add the dice onions. Let soften for 10 minutes.
  2. Add the ground beef and sausage. Increase heat to medium-high. Cook until cooked through, 8-10 minutes. Add garlic during the last minute. Drain excess grease.
  3. Add the chicken broth and use a silicone spatula to scrape the bottom and sides of the pot. Add the marinara sauce, tomato paste, hot sauce, and Worcestershire sauce. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer. Cover partially and let the sauce simmer gently.

Preheat Over and Cook Noodles

  1. Preheat oven to 375F.
  2. Begin boiling a large pot of salted water for the lasagna noodles. Once a rapid boil is reached, cook the noodles to al dente according to package instructions. (Set a timer to be safe) Gently stir with a wooden spoon throughout cooking to prevent the noodles from sticking. Drain and rinse with cold water until noodles are completely cool.
  3. Lay the cooled noodles flat on wax or parchment paper while you begin assembling the lasagna.

Assemble

  1. Spread 1 heaping cup of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 casserole dish. Next, add 4 lasagna noodles, overlapping them if necessary.
  2. spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles, followed by about 1 1/2 cups of the meat sauce.
  3. Top with 4 more lasagna noodles, 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture, 1 1/2 cups meat sauce.
  4. Finish with 4 more lasagna noodles, the rest of the ricotta mixture, and the rest of the meat sauce.
  5. Top with 2 1/2 cups of Mozzarella cheese.
  6. Spray foil with a nonstick cooking spray and place it spray-side down on top of the baking dish to prevent the cheese from sticking to it.

Bake

  1. Bake for 35 minutes. Remove cover and bake for an additional 10 minutes.
  2. Remove from the oven and garnish with fresh parsley. Let it sit for 15 minutes prior to serving to allow the layers to set. Serve with cheesy garlic bread.
